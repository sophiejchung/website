Technical Writing at Google

By Sophie Chung

Jacquard by Google is a wearables project first announced in 2015. Conductive fibers, flexible computing components as well as haptics or LEDS and software APIs are woven into garments. In one basic use-case, users can input information into their phone by touching the garment in a designated location, and can receive alerts through vibrations, sounds, or lights in the garment.

Google’s first consumer Jacquard partnership was with clothing manufacturer Levi Strauss & Co. to create Jacquard-enabled jackets, released in 2017. Most recently in October 2019, Google announced a collaboration with Levi Strauss & Co. to release a new edition of its Jacquard-enabled jackets as well as a collaboration with Yves Saint Laurent to release a Jacquard-enabled backpack.

Housed under Hardware Operations as part of the gTech Consumers unit, I headed the consumer-facing support site for Jacquard. I wrote and staged 50+ Help Content articles specifically for Jacquard in Google’s content management system. Additionally, I provided updated support content for Google Glass Enterprise and Google Pixel Buds.

You can find the live website here: https://support.google.com/jacquard

Skills: Technical writing, video script writing, project management, hardware prototype testing, data compilation and analysis, cross-functional collaboration with product managers, marketing
